Who I am

3rd year Computational Intelligence and Design student @ UBC

Hi! My name's Patrick and I am a 3rd year student at The University of British Columbia in the Cognitive Systems program. My specialty stream is Computational Intelligence and Design, where I study the intersection between machines and the human mind. I have also been a teaching assistant at UBC since my sophomore year, and I love working with students and learning alongside my peers.

Some of my technical work experience includes working as a Junior Cloud Operations Engineer at the Samsung Vancouver R&D Institute and as Technical Coordinator for the campus radio station, CiTR 101.9 FM. I also participate in various clubs and research design teams, including the UBC CTF team, Maple Bacon, and the UBC iGEM design team.

Some stuff I've done

Design + Development

I enjoy working on software projects and various computer science related topics. My particular areas of focus include algorithms, computer systems, and human-computer interaction. I enjoy working in teams big and small and a diverse range of tools, frameworks, and languages.

Teaching + Mentorship

I have been a teaching assistant at The University of British Columbia since my sophomore year and have taught three semesters of introductory programming and two semesters of introduction to cognitive science. I have also served as an academic mentor for both the UBC CS department and the Faculty of Science through UBC Peer Programs.

Research

I participate in various research initiatives at The University of British Columbia both in the Cognitive Systems department and more recently as a dry lab member of the UBC iGEM (International Genetically Engineered Machine) design team.
